Privacy Policy

StackMinds AI helps users organize brain dumps, focus plans, moods, wins, and task history.

Information We Use

The app may process brain dump text you enter so it can return organized tasks, a next step, and a focus plan.

Local Storage

Wins, mood selections, streaks, and history may be saved locally on your device using browser storage.

AI Processing

If AI mode is available, your brain dump may be sent to an AI service to generate a response. If AI is unavailable, the app uses built-in offline ADHD support.

We Do Not Sell Personal Data

We do not sell your personal information.
